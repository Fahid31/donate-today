Our Donate Plan

Donars often ask us to know about our strategy of donation. We usually work on three different
sections so that every amount put an impact on each person's life.

Food for family

There are so many families that undergo some unbearable circumstances such as lack of foods. We will provide them foods for one-month from your donation. In every week, we provides food for 1000+ families to different places

Shelter for a family

More than 9 million are homeless. They don't have a place to live. We already made shelter houseS for affected people. Also we are working to build 100+ houses for homeless. The medical boat will travel to shelters and marooned families to treat sick patients who currently do not have access to medical care

Education for a child

The life of an orphan is very miserable. Like other children, they don't get much opportunity to study. But we don't want to let them fall. To develop their educational career. Our foundation works on 5-18 years old orphans with the help of your donation.
